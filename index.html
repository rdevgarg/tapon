About TapOn

One solution to all your menstrual needs

TapOn is a non-profit app aimed at providing a safe environment to women in need of sanitary products . Our app connects you to other women who can help you when you need a sanitary napkin urgently. Get in touch with other women to talk about the problems you face during menstruation.

Our organisation will also take up volunteers who are willing to help us spread awareness to neighbouring villages and districts.
